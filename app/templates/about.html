Why make a Gratitude Journal?

Studies

show that keeping a gratitude journal is a helpful way to boost one's happiness in their lives. I believe that we should be more conscious of the things we normally take for granted. I wanted to make a public space for sharing these small things, so here we are.

Why is the app called Grats?

The name comes from "gratitude" and "congratulation", but looking back it doesn't make that much sense.
